## 🎬 CUSTOMIZING VIDEO BACKGROUND

**Why Customize?**
Video background is the first thing customers see!

**How to Change:**
1. Go to Admin Dashboard
2. Click "Settings" tab
3. Find "Hero Video URL" field
4. Go to pexels.com/videos or pixabay.com/videos
5. Search for "beauty", "salon", or "spa"
6. Download a video → Right-click → Copy download link
7. Paste link in Settings form (must be .mp4)
8. Click "SAVE"
9. Homepage updates instantly! ✨

**Free Video Sources:**
- https://www.pexels.com/videos/
- https://pixabay.com/videos/
- https://unsplash.com
